Keptly Support

Keptly is a home maintenance app that helps you track tasks by uploading PDF appliance manuals and receiving reminders.

Contact Support

If you need help, contact us at contact@getkeptly.app.

Account Deletion

If you want to delete your Keptly account, please follow the steps in the app under Settings → Account → Delete Account, or visit our account deletion page.
